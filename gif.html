Easy Peasy Activities


Abdominal Twist --- King Kong Twist

Abdominal Twist --- King Kong Twist


1. Sit in an upright position.


2. Extend the spine as twist your upper body to left and then to right.


3. Keep balance when switch sides.


4. Hold position for about 10 seconds


5. Each side 5 to 10 times


Arm Stretches --- Simba Stretches

Arm Stretches --- Simba Stretches

1. Sit in an upright position.


2. Relax the whole body. .


3. Extending the arms out in the front and interlace your fingers .


4. Keep arms stretched out.


5. Keep arms as high as possible


6. Keep these position 5 to 10 seconds


7. Repeat this exercise 5 to 10 times


Chest Squeeze With Medicine ball --- Iron man's Squeeze

Chest Squeeze With Medicine ball --- Iron man's Squeeze


1. Sit in an upright position and use your abdominal muscles to hold the back muscles.


2. Keep the rubber ball or medicine ball at the chest level .


3. Squeeze the ball using your hands and try to use your chest power .


4. Hold this position for 5 to 10 seconds


5. Rest 15 seconds and then repeat this action


6. Repeat 5 times


Neck Rolls

Neck Rolls


1. Sit in an upright position.


2. Warm up the muscles of neck by slowly moving the chin towards the chest.


3. Then, moving it your left (right) shoulder, move the neck slowly towards back, then slowly roll the head to right (left) shoulder, then back to chest.


4. Completes one cycle of anti-clockwise roll.


5. Repeat 5 times


Seated Shoulder Press --- Hulk's Superpower

Seated Shoulder Press --- Hulk's Superpower


1. Prepare a pair of dumbbells (the weight of dumbbell you are comfortable with)


2. Sit in an upright position.


3. Hold these dumbbells on either side of your head.


4. Try to push them above head while your palms facing forward, till they meet in the center.


5. Slowly bring them back into the starting position


6. Repeat 6 to 12 times a round


Shoulder Lifts --- Hello Kitty's Lifts

Shoulder Lifts --- Hello Kitty's Lifts


1. Sit in an upright position.


2. Relax the whole body.


3. Lift both your shoulders, straight up towards the ears


4. Hold for 5 to 10 seconds


5. Lower them to the starting position


6. Repeat this exercise 10 to 15 times.


Shoulder Openers --- The power of Thor

Shoulder Openers --- The power of Thor


1. Prepare a spring exerciser.


2. Sit in a comfortable posture and hold the spring exerciser at your shoulder level .


3. Try to move your arms by stretching straight to the side .


4. Squeeze the area between shoulder blades on end .


5. Repeat this 6 to 12 times.


Side Bend Stretch --- Hawkeye's Bow

Side Bend Stretch --- Hawkeye's Bow


1. Sit in an upright position.


2. Ground your hips to the wheelchair seat.


3. Extend your right arms toward the celling and bend to the left, this will make a large “C” or look like a bow.


4. Repeat the step with your left arm.


5. Each side 5 to 10 times.


Swimming --- Land Shark

Swimming --- Land Shark


1. Sit in an upright position.


2. Using both your arms and imitate swimming strokes


3. 5 to 10 times a round


4. Repeat this exercise 5 to 10 rounds
